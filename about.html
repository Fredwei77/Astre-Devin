About Us

Bridging Ancient Wisdom with Modern AI Technology

Company Introduction

Our journey began in 2012 with the establishment of Shenzhen Yimai E-commerce Development Co., Ltd. For thirteen years, we have consistently adhered to the philosophy of "user experience first," using technological innovation as our engine to provide high-value internet products to global customers.

Our mission is to provide intelligent destiny analysis, guide users to achieve inner balance, and gain a deeper understanding of their life path. Upholding the concept of "cultural globalization," we use artificial intelligence to share traditional Chinese philosophy and Eastern culture with the world.

Growth Timeline

In 2023, a competition brought several young people together. Because we all had experiences of working hard but still facing unsatisfactory outcomes in our life struggles, we got to know and cherish each other. With "Eastern Mysticism" as the starting point of destiny, we initially explored using advanced technology to revitalize traditional Eastern wisdom.

Our Team

The team members of Astre Devin come from diverse backgrounds, including top domestic universities and well-known large companies, as well as second-tier colleges and small startups. Founder & CEO

Fred Wai

Fred Wai graduated from the University of Science and Technology Beijing and has extensive professional experience. He served as Chief Operating Officer at Baby Book, a company that uses artificial intelligence technology to help law firms reduce time and labor costs. Additionally, he was a Product Manager for ByteDance's DOUBAO product. Recently, he has pioneered the application of large AI models to Chinese cultural research and broader psychological fields.

Our Vision

"Be Your Own Fate Teller"

Astre Devin hopes to help everyone achieve not falling into the abyss of emotions and blindly relying on others' interpretations, but rather to illuminate their own path forward.

We believe that the exploration of self and destiny has always been an important part of human spiritual culture. Interpreting destiny is about helping life achieve self-awareness and self-realization.

Eastern mysticism is a professional service built on a rigorous reasoning chain, which makes it naturally well-suited for enhancement through advanced reasoning models. Our technology draws from the deep domain expertise of renowned fortune-telling analysts and relies on extensive and professional datasets. Through close collaboration with these experts, we have built comprehensive knowledge graphs and developed refined algorithms. Subsequently, we created finely annotated, human-generated reasoning chain datasets that form the important foundation for model distillation and fine-tuning.

We are committed to creating positive experiences for users by prioritizing emotional value over fear-mongering, proactively connecting with users, and providing genuine companionship. To this end, we have carefully crafted the language assets of our AI agents and designed an exquisite and intuitive user interface.

We also provide natural conversational sessions with AI fortune tellers, multimodal explanations (text-to-image and text-to-video), and specialized functional modules for scenarios such as marriage matching, job hunting, college entrance exams, and career planning.
